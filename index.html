Roger That VR!

The first game I developed was 'Roger That VR!', Which is a VR local co-op sneaker game. In this game, players need to choose to play the actual invader Raven or Jackie, who control the camera from a distance to help Raven. Jackie used the camera to find the distribution of the guards in the room and told Raven the information in the room. Raven completed the actual infiltration task. In this game, I was mainly responsible for the enemy's AI and the main flow of the game. I built a AI ​​system of the guards which is similar to Assassin's Creed. They are too clever and I can only say that the sneak part of this game is thrilling and exciting.

Candle

Fortunately, I joined this year's AGP project, and our project will make a horror game. This game mainly needs to interact with the scene to trigger the plot. The part I am responsible for is the dialogue system. This is actually a complex Work, because dialogue is very important in this story-oriented game, and there are multiple mechanisms to trigger dialogue. What I do is to build a simple and easy-to-use dialogue system that is compatible with these trigger mechanisms. I think I Well done, because now if you want to insert a new plot into the game, you just need to prepare the dialogue script, and then spend 5 minutes following my dialog system tutorial. It can work!
